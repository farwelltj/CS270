Well Fed!!!

  • Screen will start to move once player has reached the half way point on the screen.
  • If you die, restart at ground
  • When bug is reached, move onto next level if and only if food metor is 100% full.
  • If bug is reached and food metor isn't full then you lose a life and have to try agian.
  • Fixed height once you are within the bug screen stops scrolling.
  • You are required to jump and stear at the same time (like Mario).
  • Line meter on side of the screen to show where you are at respect to the bug.
  • Each level is randomized.
  • Food meter increses by point value of food. The higher the meter the happier.
