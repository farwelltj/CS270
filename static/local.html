Game Jam

FEED ME!!!

Objectives

  • Collect enough food to feed the giant
  • Don't fall off the plates!
  • Don't fall behind the screen, stay infront of the bottom of the screen. You have three lives, don't die!

Details

  • 5 sec screen delay at beginning.
  • Get 3/4 way up before 5 secs, then screen starts to move.
  • If you die, restart at bottom
  • When giant is reached, move onto next level if and only if food metor is 100% full.
  • If giant is reached and food metor isn't full then you lose and are eaten by giant.
  • Fixed height once you are within the giant screen stops scrolling.
  • Start on the ground/grass screen starts moving when you want to move to the platform. Once you reach first platform screen starts moving.
  • You are required to jump and stear at the same time (like Mario).
  • Line meter on side of the screen to show where you are at respect to the giant.
  • Each level is randomized.
  • Background is different to show differences between level and level titles. Change color of plates.
  • Some plates break, bounce, float/move. Level 3 Wind gusts.

Play by Play

  • Display splash screen, press enter to play.
  • Display rules, press key to move on.
  • Player starts at the bottom.
  • Jump to first row of plates(grab food if applicable), food meterincreased.
  • Advance to different plates grabbing food as you go, increasing food meter.
  • If the bottom screen touches you, you're dead.
  • If you get to the top without a full food meter you also die.
