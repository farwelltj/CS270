Game Jam

GAME FEED ME!!!

Objectives

  • Collect enough food to feed the giant
  • Don't fall off the plates!
  • Don't fall behind the screen, stay infront of the bottom of the screen. You have three lives, don't die!

Details

5 sec screen delay at beginning. Get 3/4 way up before 5 secs, then screen starts to move. Food metor: -bread(10) -tomato(20) *food meter increses by point value of food. The higher the metor the happier -ham(30) *food metor max = 100pts -turkey(30) *each level will have at least 100 points possible. -lettuce(20) -chicken(50) If you die, restart at bottom and metor remians at the spot where you died. (Retain points until 3 lives up) When giant is reached, move onto next level if and only if food metor is 100% full. If giant is reached and food metor isn't full then you lose and are eaten by giant.

Play by Play

Display splash screen, press enter to play. Display rules, press key to move on. Player starts at the bottom. Jump to first row of plates(grab food if applicable), food meter increased. Advance to different plates grabbing food as you go, increasing food meter. If the bottom screen touches you, you're dead. If you get to the top without a full food meter you also die.
