Feed-ME!!!

The game is about a girl who is trying to climb her way up to the top of a hill while gathering food along the way to feed to a vicious bug. In order to do this she must hop from platform to platform collecting food to deliver to the bug. The meter displays how much food has been collected. When the meter is full you beat the level
