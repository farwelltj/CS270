Game Jam

FEED ME!!!

About

The game is about a girl who is trying to climb her way up to the top of a hill while gathering food along the way to feed to a viscous giant. In order to do this she must hop from platform to platform collecting sandwhich meat and bread to give to the giant. When the meter is full and displays that they she has enough food she is able to give it to the giant. However, she must face challenges along the way such as gust of wind, falling materials, and the giant himself.
